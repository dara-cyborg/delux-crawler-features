- **Service Delivery:** Providing, maintaining, and improving the App's features and functionality.

- **Analytics:** Understanding user behavior, trends, and App performance to optimize our services.
- **Communication:** Sending technical updates, security alerts, and customer support messages.
- **Marketing:** Sending promotional materials and offers (with your consent, where required).
- **Compliance:** Fulfilling legal obligations and protecting against fraud or unauthorized activity.
- **Research:** Conducting statistical analysis and research to improve our services.
